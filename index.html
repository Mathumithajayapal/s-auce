Our Craft

The Art of Fermentation

Wild fermentation isn't a shortcut — it's a commitment. We pack fresh chilis with sea salt and let native microorganisms transform them over months into something truly alive.

The result is a depth of flavour no vinegar-based sauce can replicate: complex, layered, gut-friendly, and with a slow-building heat that lingers.

01

Harvest

Hand-picked at peak ripeness from small farms across the globe.

02

Salt & Pack

Packed with unrefined sea salt in clay crocks, sealed for transformation.

03

Age & Wait

Minimum 90 days of wild fermentation. The microbes do the heavy lifting.

04

Bottle

Blended, tasted, labelled by hand. Every batch numbered and intentional.
